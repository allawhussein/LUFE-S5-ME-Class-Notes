## State Equation for Perfect Gases
$$PV=nRT=\frac{m}{M}RT=m\frac{R}{M}T=mrT$$
($N$ in $kmols$ and $m$ in $kg$)

$R$ is the universal constant for perfect gases.
$$R=8314 J.kmol^{-1}.k^{-1} \approx 8.32 J.mol^{-1}.k^{-1}$$

$PV=NRT$ (for $N$ $kmole$)  
$PV=RT$ (for $1$ $kmole$)
$PV=mrT$ (for $m$ $kg$)  
$PV=rT$ (for $1$ $kg$)
